Leadership Structure

Head of Institution (HoI)

  • Sets strategic direction and makes major corporate decisions
  • Represents the Academy at external events
  • Oversees strategic plan implementation
  • Evaluates executive leadership performance

Academic Director (AD)

  • Leads academic program development
  • Ensures academic rigor and accreditation standards
  • Oversees academic staff management
  • Coordinates curriculum alignment with industry standards

Head of Admissions and Student Services

  • Manages admissions and student records
  • Develops student recruitment strategies
  • Oversees student support services
  • Maintains student feedback systems

Head of Finance (HoF)

  • Manages financial strategy and budgets
  • Ensures regulatory compliance
  • Oversees financial reporting
  • Aligns financial management with goals

Head of Human Resources

  • Manages staff recruitment and retention
  • Develops employee policies
  • Ensures employment law compliance
  • Handles employee relations
