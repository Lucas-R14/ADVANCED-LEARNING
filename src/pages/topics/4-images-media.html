Images & Media

Guidelines and best practices for visual content at Advanced Learning Academy

Role of Images & Media

Visual content is essential for effective communication, engagement, and learning at Advanced Learning Academy. We use images, videos, and other media to enhance educational materials, promote our brand, and create an inclusive, dynamic environment for all learners.

Usage Guidelines

  • Use high-quality, relevant images and media that support the content and learning objectives.
  • Ensure all media is properly licensed or owned by the Academy. Avoid copyright infringement.
  • Follow branding guidelines for logos, colors, and visual identity.
  • Obtain written permission for any identifiable individuals featured in photos or videos.
  • Use descriptive alt text for all images to support accessibility.

Types of Media

Photographs

Used for events, campus life, and promotional materials. Must be high-resolution and reflect the Academy's values.

Videos

Educational lectures, promotional clips, and student testimonials. Videos should be clear, well-edited, and include captions.

Infographics

Visual representations of data, processes, or concepts. Should be accurate, branded, and easy to understand.

Presentations

Slides and digital presentations for classes and events. Use Academy templates and maintain visual consistency.

Media Management & Storage

All official media assets are stored in secure, organized repositories managed by the Academy's communications team. File names should be descriptive, and folders should be structured by event, date, or project for easy retrieval.

Accessibility & Optimization

  • Provide alt text for all images and transcripts or captions for videos.
  • Compress images and videos to ensure fast loading without sacrificing quality.
  • Use accessible color contrasts and readable fonts in all media.

Permissions & Copyright

Only use media for which the Academy holds the rights or has obtained proper licenses. Always credit external sources when required and respect copyright laws. For questions about usage rights, contact the communications team.

Contact for Media Inquiries

For questions about media usage, permissions, or to request official assets, please contact: media@advancedlearning.edu
