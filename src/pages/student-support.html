Policies & Procedures

We are committed to maintaining a fair and supportive learning environment. Below are our key policies and procedures.

Academic Appeals

If you believe there has been an error in an academic decision, you may submit an appeal.

Grounds for appeal include:

  • Procedural error in marking or moderation
  • New evidence not previously available
  • Concerns about bias or conflict of interest

Appeals must be submitted in writing within 10 working days of the decision.

Accessibility & Inclusion

We are committed to removing barriers to learning and providing equal opportunities for all students.

Available accommodations include:

  • Accessible learning materials
  • Support plans for disabilities
  • Adjusted assessment methods
  • Assistive technology support

Complaints Procedure

We take all complaints seriously and are committed to resolving them fairly and efficiently.

Our process:

  1. Informal resolution with the relevant person
  2. Formal complaint submission if unresolved
  3. Appeal process if dissatisfied with the outcome

Formal complaints should be submitted within 15 working days of the issue arising.
